Welcome to Dreamscape One


Dreamscape One is a sustainable, generational global project related to Climate Change. DS1 Center Piece is a Fully Enclosed Superstructure with a minimum roof height of 160 feet built on a footprint of 500 acres. It sits within the TEN THOUSAND ACRE (10,000) CITY known as DS1.
